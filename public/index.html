If you want to help make Leela Chess Zero stronger, please think about contributing your GPU and/or CPU at http://lczero.org/ !

What are modes? Modes are leela playing at different nodes, which are similar to classical nodes in traditional chess engines.
Easy mode is special because Leela is not doing any calculations and is playing purely by insight, just taking the board and evaluating what looks interesting.
Other modes are just about adding nodes, thus making deeper calculations and checking tactics.

Tip: To play as black, just flip the board and "Force Leela Move" to make her play first.
